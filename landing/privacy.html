Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

uboth collects only what's needed for the app to function: your name, your paired person's identifier, session timestamps, and mood check-ins shared during sessions. This data is stored in Firebase and is only accessible to you and your paired person.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to run the app — syncing sessions in real time, maintaining your pairing, and storing your shared history. We do not analyze, profile, or use your data for any other purpose.

What we don't do

We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal information with any third party. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or any form of behavioral tracking.

Authentication

uboth uses Apple Sign-In for authentication. We receive only the information Apple provides: a unique user identifier and, optionally, your name. We do not receive your Apple ID email unless you choose to share it.

Data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at outsidevibras@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

Questions? Reach us at outsidevibras@gmail.com.
